WHO ARE WE

ABOUT US

A decade ago Ryan was in Bangkok coding and teaching at a design agency; Warren, a young medical doctor, was treating casualty patients in a Cape Town hospital; and in another hemisphere, Sophia was in a suit trying to figure out what an international relations graduate was doing at a major management consulting firm.

10 years, 19 countries, 16 different job descriptions, 31 clients, 2 MBA’s, 1 Design Thinking degree, and a few big career moves later…Think 2099 was born.

If a doctor, a tech entrepreneur with an eye for design, and a reformed management consultant can be stronger together, so can you. When you combine multiple perspectives, analytical rigour, and deep industry knowledge, you can transform insights into impactful solutions.

OUR APPROACH

1. Empathize

How well do you know your end user?

2. Ideate

Have you created the time and a space free of judgement to think of all possible solutions, ideas and concepts related to your challenge?

3. Define

Are you solving a problem or a symptom? Have you gotten to the root problem you are trying to solve?

4. Test

Have you validated the assumptions you have about your product/service with the people using it?

Warren Gatcke

"The Strategist"

Warren has an MD and MBA from UCT. His background practicing medicine built a people focus into his DNA that is intrinsic to developing products and solutions centered around real human needs. Training from the Cape Town D-School allowed him hone those skills. Warren brings a uniquely informed perspective to any problem/challenge.

Ryan Byrne

"The Designer"

Ryan has a strong technical background and is one of the first students in Africa to hold a BDes with specialization in ideation. He also has a passion for design and creative arts. This combination brings a unique perspective to problem solving and a strong execution capability.

Sophia Campello Beckwith

"The Do-er"

Sophia is a Brasilian-American ideator who made Africa her forever home. She is a reformed management consultant with extensive experience building businesses, programs and communities in Sub-Saharan Africa. She is equally at home in a boardroom as on a farm, and has a passion for sustainability, inclusivity and agriculture.
